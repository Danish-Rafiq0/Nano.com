Additives

Paint Coatings includes four major components, namely resins/binders, Additives, Solvents, and Pigments. Additives in paints, also known as coating auxiliary materials, play a crucial role in enhancing coating performance and facilitating film formation.

Pigment Based Paste

Pigment Pastes are colored liquid/paste mediums based on pigments dispersed with monomer-free/solvent-free resin systems. They are available in a wide range of colors varying in strength and consistency depending upon the particular coloring application.

PRODUCT CATEGORIES

Additives

Normally, additives can be used in coating, with the content ranging from 0.01% to 5% of the formulation. Additives have different structures and physicochemical properties, and many of them are diverse types of surfactants. They can either work together synergistically or counteract each other in paints.
